MORE How It Works

Police alarm response over the years has been deprioritized due to the high percentage of false alarms within the security industry. Most false alarms are caused by user error and in some cases improper installation of detection devices by installers that are not properly trained. This results in the regional police forces placing alarm services at the lower priority or not responding at all.


This lower priority means that if you have an actual emergency, the response by the police may be longer then an acceptable timeframe. The police will usually respond faster if the they have a verified personal injury alarm.


The importance of RSPNDR has never been more critical. Your can now benefit from this new platform with faster response time, verified perimeter checks of our premises and a detailed report with response times.
