Commercial Security Systems

Security Solutions for any business can get confusing. The risk factors of securing a business goes much further then installing a security system to prevent intrusions.

Proper security of a business also helps owners and managers to


    1: Reduce shrinkage

    2: Protect loss during operational hours

    3: Protect front line staff against aggressive clients

    4: Protect the company against disgruntled employees

    5: Protection against insurance claims


Palladium Security understands that every business requires different level of security, Solutions we offer can be as simple as installation of a perimeter protection to a more advanced integrated solution that encompasses security, Access control, Closed circuit video and automation.


We will discuss with you to determine the level of security you require and then submit a solution that we feel is the best possible product that will fit your needs. Click on the new security system link below for more details

Integrated Security Solutions

Integrated Security Solutions for either your home or business can be customized to suite your needs.  The solutions can be as easy and adding simple lighting automation to light up a pathway when you enter your home to something that will allow you to send video verifications to your smartphone if the alarm is triggered.  


We understand that not all clients require the same solutions so we try to build your integration system in a way that helps simplify your life but not break the vault.
